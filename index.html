THE ARTIST

KAREEM OLUWATOMI LAWAL

Base: Abuja, Nigeria

Boy Geezii is not just an artist; he is a movement. Known as "The Renegade," he blends the ancient storytelling traditions of African folklore with the gritty, neon-soaked realities of modern Afro-Fusion and Hip-Hop.

From the soulful depths of "Letters To You" to the high-energy vibrations of "Terribull @ Gudbuys," his sound is a distinct fusion of World Music and Rap. He crafts sonic landscapes that explore resilience, love, and the complex "nomad's rhythm" of life.
