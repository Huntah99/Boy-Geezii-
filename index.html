01 // THE RENEGADE

Kareem Oluwatomi Lawal

Base: Abuja, Nigeria | Active Since: 2019

Boy Geezii is an Afro-Fusion and Hip-Hop artist known for his unique "Renegade" sound. Blending World Music folklore with gritty rap and melodic rhythms, he creates sonic landscapes that tell stories of resilience, love, and the hustle.

With major projects like Cungrachulayshuns X Kondolenses and Terribull @ Gudbuys, Geezii continues to push the boundaries of the Nigerian soundscape.
